About Us

Rookies Animation Studio

ROOKIES is an animation studio dedicated to creating and distributing high-quality animated films and short stories that captivate audiences of all ages. Alongside our productions, we offer mentorship programs to nurture aspiring filmmakers and animators, helping them bring their creative visions to life. With a unique blend of innocence and professionalism, ROOKIES aims to deliver stories that inspire, entertain, and connect with people everywhere.

Our Objectives

ROOKIES aims to create top-tier animated content that captivates and inspires, providing valuable mentorship opportunities for aspiring animators. By fostering a collaborative environment and nurturing talent, we strive to position ourselves as a leading studio in the animation industry. Through our commitment to innovation and storytelling, we aim to build long-lasting relationships with our audiences, partners, and collaborators, leaving a lasting impact on the world of animation.

The Vision and Mission

ROOKIES aims to inspire and entertain audiences of all ages through exceptional animated stories while shaping the future of storytelling by empowering aspiring filmmakers and animators and fostering creativity worldwide.

  • At ROOKIES, we are dedicated to crafting heartwarming animated stories that inspire joy and connection across generations while empowering aspiring filmmakers and animators to bring their creative visions to life.
  • To become a globally recognized leader in animation, known for creating timeless stories and fostering a new generation of innovative filmmakers who enrich the world with their artistry and imagination.
